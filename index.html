About Dr Nut

Dr. Nut is more than just a drink; it's a New Orleans legend. Originally crafted in the early 20th century, our signature almond-flavored soda became a cultural icon known for its unique taste and its famous squirrel mascot. After decades of refreshing generations, the legend is back to bring that classic, intense almond flavor to a new era of energy and refreshment.
